Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect while you use MagicFrame, how we use, share, and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data

We may collect the following personal data when you use MagicFrame:

  • Subscription and purchase history (processed through third-party services such as RevenueCat)

1.2 Image and Biometric Data

To provide our services, we collect and process:

  • Facial images and limited biometric data provided by the user for AI-powered photo and video generation.

These data are used solely for the following purposes:

  • Baby prediction
  • Face swapping
  • Template-based image insertion
  • Animation creation

To enhance the user experience, previously uploaded faces may be made accessible again through sections like "Recent". These facial data are stored exclusively for the respective user and are not shared with other users. All such data are collected and processed only with the user's explicit consent within the app.

1.3 Device and Usage Data

We may also collect the following non-personal data:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Language preferences
  • App usage statistics (via Firebase Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data are used for the following purposes:

  • To provide AI-powered photo and video generation services
  • To enhance and personalize the user experience
  • To analyze usage trends and optimize the app
  • To manage in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud

3. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share data in the following cases:

  • Service providers: AI processing partners and cloud storage services
  • Payment processors: Secure payment processing through third-party services
  • Legal authorities: When required to comply with legal obligations

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to deliver our services. Facial images and biometric data are retained on our systems for a maximum of 30 days. This period is defined to allow users to revisit their latest uploads, maintain technical continuity of the service, and ensure a smooth user experience. After this period, the data are automatically and permanently deleted from our systems or anonymized.

Users may delete their data at any time within the app. Once deleted, all facial data are permanently removed from our servers.

Facial data may be transmitted in an anonymous and temporary manner to specific AI service providers solely for the purpose of generating visual content. These data do not contain personal identifiers and are used only for the intended processing. The handling of such data is also subject to the privacy policies of the respective service providers.

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

5. User Rights and Control

Users have the right to access, view, or permanently delete the facial data they have uploaded. These actions can easily be performed via the “Delete My Data” feature within the app.

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Request the deletion of your biometric data

6. Children’s Privacy

MagicFrame is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 13.

7. Third-Party Services

MagicFrame integrates the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat (Subscription and payment management)
  • AI service providers (Image processing and generation)
  • Firebase Analytics (User analytics and behavior tracking)

Each service provider manages data usage under their own privacy policies.

Facial data are transmitted only temporarily and anonymously to the AI services we use for image generation (e.g., image generation API providers). No personal or identifying information is shared with these services; only the necessary visual data is transmitted.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be accessible within the app.
